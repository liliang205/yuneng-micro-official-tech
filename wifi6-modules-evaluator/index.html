WiFi6 (QCN9024) Recommended

At 1km outdoor bridge with 50 users, WiFi6 delivers 380 Mbps UDP throughput (37% higher than WiFi5) and 8 ms latency (75% lower). With 50% WiFi6-capable clients, OFDMA and MU-MIMO benefits scale proportionally. Estimated 5-year TCO savings are significant due to 40% fewer APs and lower maintenance costs.
