## Article Summary

Who this is for:Wearable device manufacturers, AR/VR headset makers, premium tablet OEMs, and ODM partners requiring ultra-compact WiFi solutions.

Core problem:Next-generation wearables and AR/VR devices demand WiFi connectivity in form factors measured in millimeters, which traditional modules can’t accommodate.

### Key Technical Takeaways

- 3D Stacking:Vertical integration of WiFi/Bluetooth IC, antenna, and passives
- Integrated Antenna:Ceramic antenna embedded in package
- Substrate Integration:RF matching components built into substrate
- Multi-Chip Module:WiFi + Bluetooth + MCU in single package
- Embedded Passives:Resistors, capacitors integrated into substrate
